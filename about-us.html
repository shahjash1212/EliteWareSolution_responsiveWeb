Our Journey

2016

A young dynamic individual Jigar Shah decided to quit his Job from TCS and took a challenge for entrepreneurial journey by establishing Eliteware Solutions in 2016 with a vision to help growing startups by providing excellent and affordable IT services.

2018

Eliteware Solutions with its one year of inception helped businesses across the world in becoming successful. 2 new products were launched by end of 2018 viz. CRM and ERP solution for Contractors.

2019

By 2019, Eliteware Solutions grew its footprint across 15+ countries by serving clients across multiple industries ranging from Manufacturing, Staffing, Contractors and E-Commerce.

2020

With pandemic hit across the world, Eliteware Solutions utilised this time to research and learn about different domains and expanded our expert areas to include UI/UX, Digital Marketing, Cloud Computing and IOT.
